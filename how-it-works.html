Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
How to get cashback for my purchase?
Follow below steps to get cashback for all your purchases:
  1. 1. Sign up/Login on BonusApp
  2. 2. Select the store & the offer
  3. 3. Click “Shop Now” or “show code” or “grab the deal” (Give your email if you are not already logged in)
  4. 4. Complete the purchase from the store
  5. 5. Cashback will be credited in 3-4 days after your purchase
How many days will it take to get cashback for my purchase?
Cashback will be credited in 3-4 days after your purchase from the store. Please note that the cashback credited in your account will be verified in 4-12 weeks after confirming from the store for returns/cancellations (if applicable). Once verified, Cashback can be transferred to your Bank A/c or redeemed for Paytm cash/Amazon Gift Vouchers.
How will I receive my cashback?
Cashback from BonusApp is real cash that you can transfer to your Bank A/c or redeem against Paytm cash / Amazon gift vouchers after verification
After how many days, I can use my cashback?
Cashback will get verified in 4-12 weeks after your purchase. The delay is because the store has to confirm returns/cancellations (if applicable) with us which is usually done by the store in 4-12 weeks.
I have done my purchase but haven’t received my cashback?
Ideally, you should be able to see the cashback amount against your purchase in 3-4 days. In case you haven’t received cashback for your purchase, you can raise claim from Cashback Activity page. Click here to raise claim now.
How to check cashback for my purchases?
You can check the cashback credited for all your purchases under 'Cashback Earned' tab of your Cashback account page. You can login or sign up using the same email that you have used for your purchases. Click here to check your account now.
